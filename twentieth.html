Moshii, you are my safe space and my greatest motivation. I promise to keep working hard for us, to keep loving you through the distance, and to keep holding onto the hope that soon, the miles between us won't matter anymore — because we will finally be together.

I love you more than words can say. Here's to many more years of choosing each other, no matter what. 💋
